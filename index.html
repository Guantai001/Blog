About Us

Who We Are.

CTRL-PR gets your story out there. We are a PR Agency specializing in the development of modern and innovative PR solutions for startup businesses around the world. Whoever you want to influence, we help you to change the way customers see your brand. We cover a variety of domains, this includes but not limited to tech, Web3(blockchain and cryptocurrencies), retail businesses, financial institutions, B2B companies, fashion and lifestyle..

How We Work

Our team of PR professionals will work with you to develop and convey your brand’s story to your target audience. We have a few different strategies to do this. First, we craft the best angle to tell your story. Then we make pitch to specific writers who are our close personal contacts and who we find are exactly right for you. With the professional content writer, we then make sure to select the right media outlets to capture your audience’s attention.
